BENEFITS OF FRENCH FRIES

French fries often are criticized as unhealthy. But new research shows that the
food provides health benefits, according to the the Alliance for Potato Research
and Education. “French fries, as part of mixed meals, produce lower blood
glucose and insulin levels in children,” the organization says.
